## New mental model synthesis

### Composable BIM atoms

BIM 2.0 may not begin for Rhino-heavy practices as a new model platform. It may begin as small, native, editable atoms:

1. a generated Grasshopper definition;
2. a selected-object classifier;
3. a property/user-text pack;
4. an IDS-lite check;
5. a BCF-lite issue card;
6. a tiny project-memory update.

This combines Hypar’s “smallest useful BIM” with Raven’s editable GH generation and Rhino Mind’s semantic sidecar thesis.
