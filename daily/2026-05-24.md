## New mental model synthesis

### MCP is the socket; semantics are the product

MCP gives agents a way to call tools, but the durable value for Rhino Mind will come from the AEC-specific layer above raw calls:

1. **Design-environment adapter** — RhinoMCP-style primitives: create/select/edit geometry, inspect layers, capture viewport.
2. **Semantic model adapter** — IFC/Speckle/bSDD/IDS-style primitives: inspect object classes/properties, classify elements, check requirements.
3. **Collaboration adapter** — BCF/OpenCDE-style primitives: turn findings into reviewable issues with context, viewpoints, responsible party, and status.
4. **Evidence adapter** — provenance: what was checked, what changed, what uncertainty remains.

This reframes Rhino Mind from “agent controls Rhino” to “agent turns design context into checkable, shareable project intelligence.”
