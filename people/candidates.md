# BIM 2.0 People Candidates

Status: live longlist. Last updated: 2026-05-24.

Add candidates here before promotion to `people/top-100.md`.

The important unit is not just “person with influence”. It is:

**person → artifact trail → mental model → why it matters → what new model/action Rhino Mind can derive.**

Every strong candidate should have a mental-model note, even if rough.
