## Research procedure for ATN/YouTube sources

For each company/interview:

1. Capture video ID, title, date if available, guests, company, and ATN host.
2. Fetch transcript if possible. If transcript is blocked by YouTube/cloud IP, use title, chapters, description snippets, official company site/docs, and follow-up web/GitHub sources; record the transcript-block caveat.
3. Extract the founder/company mental model.
4. Identify product wedge: what task/workflow do they start with?
5. Identify implementation evidence: demo, docs, API, GitHub, integrations, customers, changelog, job posts.
6. Identify adoption friction: who has to change workflow, data model, liability pattern, or authoring tool?
7. Add people to candidates/top-100 only when the person carries a distinct, actionable mental model.
8. Add companies to company landscape even if person evidence is not yet enough for Top 100.
