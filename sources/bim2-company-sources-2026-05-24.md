## Ranked / clustered company list

### Tier 1 — specific BIM 2.0 / Rebuilding BIM authoring competitors

1. **Snaptrude** — **BIM 2.0 core**
   - Evidence: ATN BIM 2.0 overview and Snaptrude episode; AEC “Rebuilding BIM: Snaptrude”; AEC “Snaptrude AI: conceptual design and beyond”; primary site says “AI-Powered BIM software”.
   - Notes: Browser-native BIM and AI-assisted concept/schematic-to-BIM workflow. One of the clearest direct “next BIM authoring platform” companies.

2. **Arcol** — **BIM 2.0 core**
   - Evidence: ATN “Figma of AEC” episode; AEC “Arcol unleashed – BIM 2.0”; AEC “Rebuilding BIM: Arcol”; primary site describes connected massing, floorplans, metrics, estimating, documentation.
   - Notes: Web/collaborative, constructible design authoring. Strong “Figma for AEC/BIM” positioning.

3. **Motif** — **BIM 2.0 core, but current product wedge is collaboration/review**
   - Evidence: ATN “Disrupt BIM” episode; AEC “Rebuilding BIM: Motif”; AEC “Motif V1”; primary site describes intelligent browser workspace for architects.
   - Notes: Very strong BIM 2.0 thesis/founder narrative; visible product is currently review/3D streaming/AI visualisation/collaboration more than full BIM authoring.

4. **Qonic** — **BIM 2.0 core, construction/detail BIM orientation**
   - Evidence: ATN Qonic x Giraffe workshop; AEC “Rebuilding BIM: Qonic”; AEC articles on drawing generation, construction BIM, Atom IFC component-level BIM data sharing; primary site: cloud BIM modelling platform.
   - Notes: More explicit BIM modelling/data than many peers; strong open/IFC-ish signal via component-level sharing.

### Tier 2 — design automation / AI-to-BIM / schematic-to-BIM

5. **Hypar** — **BIM 2.0 adjacent-to-core automation platform**
   - Evidence: ATN Hypar episode; AEC “Hypar’s big bet against schema”, “Hypar 2.0”, “Hypar: text-to-BIM and beyond”; primary site: Revit-compatible building design.
   - Notes: Less “new Revit UI”, more function/API/generative system for building design. Important for agentic/tooling-on-demand mental model.

6. **Skema** — **BIM 2.0 adjacent/core for BIM workflow compression**
   - Evidence: AEC “BIM workflow compression with Skema”; AEC “Skema.ai conceptual design and re-use engine for Revit”; primary site: AI design assistant for schematic-to-coordinated BIM.
   - Notes: Strong practical wedge: compress schematic-to-Revit/BIM using firm knowledge. Very relevant to Agents of Architecture despite not being in ATN’s original 9.

7. **Finch / Finch3D** — **adjacent early-design AI, not full BIM 2.0 authoring**
   - Evidence: ATN Finch episode; AEC Finch AI coverage; primary site: AI + graph technology for design optimisation and error detection.
   - Notes: Strong AI design feedback and floor-plan optimisation; classify as early-stage design intelligence rather than full BIM replacement.

8. **Augmenta** — **adjacent generative building systems / MEP automation**
   - Evidence: AEC “Augmenta’s productivity promise”; primary site: autonomous building design with spatial AI, currently electrical routing.
   - Notes: Discipline-specific automation. Important next-gen BIM ingredient but not general BIM authoring.

9. **SWAPP** — **adjacent automated BIM/CD production**
   - Evidence: AEC “Swapp: the algorithmic assistant”; primary site currently Cloudflare-blocked from this environment.
   - Notes: Automated documentation/BIM production. Relevant but needs more primary verification.

## Practical interpretation for Agents of Architecture / Rhino Mind

- The strongest “BIM 2.0” comparables are **Snaptrude, Arcol, Motif, Qonic**.
- The strongest “agentic/AI BIM workflow” comparables are **Hypar, Skema, SWAPP, Augmenta, RunChat**.
- The strongest “semantic/project data infrastructure” comparables are **Speckle, That Open Company, IfcOpenShell/Bonsai**.
- The strongest “upstream feasibility intelligence” comparables are **Giraffe, TestFit, Finch**.
- Do not treat all of these as the same category: many are AEC AI/design-tech companies, but only a subset are explicitly trying to rebuild BIM authoring itself.
